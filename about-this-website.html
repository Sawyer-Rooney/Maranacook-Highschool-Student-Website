What is this Website?

The purpose of this website is to keep track of everything there is to know about our school, Maranacook! Included on this website are current school events, such as theater productions, major sports events, or our yearly Winter Carnival competitions! Also included on this website are scans of our student yearbooks from previous years, information about student leaders from different student groups, and regularly updated student shoutouts!

Who Maintains It?

The management of this website is the responsibility of the technology committee within the student government. However, that is not all! The photos, videos, yearbook scans, and event information are all gathered from a list of students and faculty from across our school. If you would like to know more information about how we keep this website updated, please be sure to email our technology committee head, currently: sawyerrooney@maranacook.com

Who Created It?

This website was initially created in 2026 by Maranacook Alumnus Sawyer Rooney. Sawyer was on the Maranacook Student Council and Government for three years between 2023 and 2026, and was a member of the Technology Committee. As head of the Technology Committee in 2026, he wanted to contribute something to the school in perpetuity, so he programmed this website largely from scratch using GitHub pages.

If this website has technical issues, glitches, or needs assistance with updating information, please do not hesitate to email him at sawyerrooney3@gmail.com, and he will do his best to assist!
